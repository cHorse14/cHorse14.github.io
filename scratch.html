Why I Like the Knicks

  1. First Round
    • The Knicks could play the Heat or the 76ers, depending on who wins the play in game.
    • The Knicks are just as physical as the Heat, thus mitigating the advatage the Heat hold over the rest of the Eastern Conference.
    • The Knicks match up well against Joel Embiid, thus presenting a challegne for the 76ers offense.
  2. Second Round
    • Honestly, I can see the Pacers vs. Bucks series going either way, but I think the Bucks will win. They have much better defense than the Pacers.
    • Regardless of whether they play the Bucks or Pacers, the Knicks are just too complete of a team on offense and defense. The Bucks are a mess and the Pacers can't play defense, giving the Knicks an obvious leg up in this series.
  3. Conference Finals
    • There is no chance that the Celtics will be eliminated before the conference finals. They're just too good.
    • The Knicks will play the Celtics, and this can go either way. I'd love to see the Knicks go to the NBA Finals, but we will have to see.
    • Jalen Bruson is going to ball out regardless.
